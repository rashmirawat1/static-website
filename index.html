Attack on Titan - The Epic Saga of Humanity's Fight

Plot Summary

In a world where humanity hides behind massive walls to escape man-eating Titans, young Eren Yeager dreams of freedom beyond the barriers. When the Colossal Titan breaches Wall Maria in 845, Eren's hometown is destroyed, and his mother is devoured. Vowing revenge, Eren joins the Survey Corps with Mikasa Ackerman and Armin Arlert. Secrets about Titans and Eren's powers unfold, leading to a global war between Eldians and Marley, culminating in the Rumbling.
