Attack on Titan (Shingeki no Kyojin)

A dark fantasy anime about survival, revenge, and hidden truths.

Plot Summary

In a world where humanity hides behind massive walls to escape man-eating Titans, young Eren Yeager dreams of freedom beyond the barriers. When the Colossal Titan breaches Wall Maria in 845, Eren's hometown is destroyed, and his mother is devoured. Vowing revenge, Eren joins the Survey Corps with childhood friends Mikasa Ackerman and Armin Arlert. As they uncover secrets about the Titans—including Eren's ability to transform into one—the story expands into a global conflict between Eldians and Marley, revealing cycles of hatred, power, and the Rumbling apocalypse.

Main Characters

  • Eren Yeager: The determined protagonist who becomes a Titan shifter, driven by rage and a quest for freedom.
  • Mikasa Ackerman: Eren's fiercely protective adoptive friend (lover), a master swordswoman with unmatched strength.
  • Armin Arlert: The strategic genius of the group, evolving from timid to a key leader.
  • Levi Ackerman: Humanity's strongest soldier, a cold, skilled captain in the Survey Corps.
  • Hange Zoë: Eccentric scientist obsessed with Titan research, later Survey Corps commander.
  • Erwin Smith: Charismatic commander whose sacrifices drive the fight for truth.
